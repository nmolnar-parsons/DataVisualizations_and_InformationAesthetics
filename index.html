Obsdian Wine Company Harvests, 2015 to 2024

visualizing harvests and fire disruption

Data from Obsidian Wine Company internal financial report, not publically available

Obsidian Wine Company is a Northern California Winery which owns and operates two vineyards: Obsidian Ridge Vineyard, in Lake County, and Poseidon Vineyard, in Napa County. Grapes harvested from each vineyard are weighed and processed into wine at Obsidian's Winery.

This radial stacked area chart visualizes the tons of grapes harvested from Obsidian and Poseidon vineyards from 2015 to 2024. Vineyards are harvested once a year, usually between the months of August and Novemeber. Fires in 2018 (Mendocino Complex) and 2020 (LNU Lighting Complex) severely disrupted wine harvesting in Lake County: grapes were "smoke tainted" and were not fit to be harvested and processed into wine. This graph highlights the disruption of the fires on harvests in this decade.
